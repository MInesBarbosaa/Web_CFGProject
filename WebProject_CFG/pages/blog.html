The Magic of the Northern Lights

The northern lights (Aurora borealis) aren't exactly a place on Earth. They are a naturally occurring atmospheric phenomenon that can happen in many places around the Northern hemisphere, between 60 and 75 degrees of latitude. They are a dancing frenzy between the goodess of dawn, Aurora, and Boreas, the northern wind.
